About YCH

YCH Consumer Goods Trading is the evolution of Hank & Co., built on a foundation of trust, compliance, and innovation. We specialize in government procurement, distributor partnerships, and festive corporate gifting solutions. Our mission is to empower businesses with competitive, culturally attuned, and resilient strategies that drive growth across industries.

From IT and education to industrial and military sectors, YCH provides procurement solutions that meet the highest standards. At the same time, we bring Filipino heritage and creativity into our seasonal gifting products, blending professionalism with cultural pride.

Guided by resilience and a commitment to excellence, YCH continues to expand its reach, ensuring that every client receives not only products and services, but also a partnership built for long‑term success.
